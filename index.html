🛒 Smart Shopping Tips

💡 Buy in Bulk

Red lentils, rice, oats, and dried beans are cheapest per serving. Visit Bulk Barn on Prospect St — bring your own containers to save more.

🏷️ Store Brands

No Name & store-brand canned goods at Superstore, Sobeys & Walmart cost significantly less than name brands with identical nutrition. Always check the unit price label.

📅 Boyce Farmers Market

Open Saturdays year-round in downtown Fredericton. End-of-day produce often sold at reduced prices — great for local and Indigenous-grown foods.

📱 Use Flyer Apps

Download Flipp or Reebee to compare weekly flyers from all Fredericton grocery stores before shopping. Plan meals around what's on sale.

🥶 Frozen = Smart

Frozen vegetables and berries retain nearly all nutrients of fresh and cost far less. Frozen spinach, peas, mixed veg, and blueberries are essential pantry staples.

🥚 Budget Proteins

Eggs, red lentils, black beans, and canned chickpeas are the most affordable protein sources in Fredericton. Stock these pantry staples always.
